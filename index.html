About Me

Hi! I’m Sena, an enthusiastic designer with a deep love for creativity in all its forms. Whether it’s a website, poster, book cover, or animation, I find joy in bringing ideas to life through design.

Ever since I was a child, I’ve loved creating patterns and experimenting with colors — I actually thought I’d become a fashion designer one day. But when I discovered computers, everything changed.

My Process

Before I start designing anything — whether it’s a website or a simple graphic — I always begin with a rough sketch. I map out the layout, color ideas, and animations on paper first. Once that vision feels right, I move to the digital stage, where the real magic begins.

For me, design is like a puzzle: once you know what you want to create, everything falls into place.
